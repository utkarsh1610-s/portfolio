NLP Research

LLM Sycophancy Detection Under Pressure

Hidden state extraction across all transformer layers for 5 architectures on a 150+ question, 5-level pressure benchmark. Cosine similarity disruption at the first pressure turn predicts behavioural flip 3–4 turns in advance without probe training. RBF SVM & KNN achieved 75.5% accuracy (+11.7% above chance). A dissociation between Layer 28 dominance in Qwen-7B and layers 17–19 global predictiveness — a finding unreported in sycophancy literature.
